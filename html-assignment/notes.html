Tags

These are some of the tags that I have learned:

<html></html>
The most important tag on our page, anything we include must be inside it
<img>
The image tag: Displays an image on the webpage, it is a singular tag.
<a></a>
The anchor tag: For creating a link on our page
<form></form>
Adds a form to the HTML page
<table></table>
Creates a table container
